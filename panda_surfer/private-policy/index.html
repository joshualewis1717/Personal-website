How to delete your information?

In Short: We do not store your data, however you can revoke consent and delete or reset your advertising id

We (lewis coding) do not store or have access to any of your data, it is passed directly to the advertiser for adverts to be served. To delete your data, you must revoke consent from the app using the review consent choices button on the about page. To reset or delete your devices personalised advertising id, follow the steps listed below:

  • On your Android device, go to Settings
  • Tap Privacy > Ads.
  • Tap Reset or Delete Advertising ID and confirm your changes.

Please note: This action isn't specific to our app and will affect all other apps on your device. Whilst your advertising ID will be deleted, other apps may have their own settings which affect the types of ads you see. For more information about your advertising id, please click here

What local data does this app store on my device?

In Short: Small text files are used to save the app's configuration, and are stored under the it's package name so that they are deleted automatically when the app is uninstalled

The only files this app generates and stores on your device which are not created during the app's original installation, are small text files containing your preferred settings. For example, if you change the playback speed in the app's settings page, a file containing your new preferred value is created and stored under the app's package name. This is done so that when the app is closed and reopened, the setting can be reloaded. Furthermore, because the files are stored under the apps package name, your phone will automatically delete them when the app is uninstalled.
